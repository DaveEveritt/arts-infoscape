Colour of large spheres

range of influence the organisation has:

  • green = local
  • blue = regional
  • red = national
  • purple = international

yellow cube or violet pyramid = organisations that have hosted activity linked to one of the featured disability-specific organisations.

Shape

kind of organisation:

  • sphere (various colours) = featured disability arts/arts&disability organisation
  • pyramid (violet) = linked educational establishment (linked by green lines)
  • cube (yellow) = linked organisation (linked by red lines)

Unique Qualities

Staff: We offer people the chance to experience their creative potential. We work in a proper arts environment with professional artists. We aim to work in partnership with all sectors including Lifelong learning providers, Arts organisations, Service providers and other similar groups. The participants have complete control of their work. As well as their artistic development the projects see people develop their self-esteem, confidence and team work. It is a social opportunity. We also encourage and are working towards mentoring and supporting participants who wish to consider the arts as a career option.

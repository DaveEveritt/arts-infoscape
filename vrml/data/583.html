Unique Qualities

Staff: The organisations values make People express unique and they are reflected in its actual day to day work. For example, valuing people, listening to people, valuing what they have to say and acting on it. It is an open organisation , who work together like a family and where everyone gets valued.
People Express uncover the creativity and artistic talent of people in South Derbyshire. The work we do is unique because it is about where we are and the people who live there; the work is reflective of South Derbyshire.

Board: The committed and knowledgeable staff and highly supportive management committee. Together they work to meet their aims and clearly defined needs. People Express do not respond to every request but are more focussed. The established relationships with the artists working for them mean the artists work with commitment and excitement delivering exciting and cutting edge projects. The new training programme will provide People Express with a unique pool of trained local artsworkers and managers.

Linked: There are 2 key things:|1.They target their work at disadvantaged groups or communities.|2.Their community development approach.

Participant: People express are the only arts organisation I have really been in contact with, but we all seem to work well together. Everyone is really friendly and with their help we are now able to raise money ourselves. People Express have helped to move us on to becoming more independent and building our confidence. Our success feeds their success and visa versa, the relationship is very equal.

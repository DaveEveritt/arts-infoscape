Unique Qualities

Staff: I think because it has emerged from the user group rather than people coming in from outside - I think that's what makes it unique. There's a lot of creative art work goes on but not a lot that is managed by and involved with the actual users.

Board: It is led by mental health users. The group that is serves, and not by anyone with preconceptions of what mental health is or how it should be treated or portrayed.

Participant: I think because it comes so much from the personal and people are experiencing and writing about the problems they have as part of their way of life.
